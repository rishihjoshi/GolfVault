Process risks in the current workflow
Status updates lag 1–24 hours after actual fulfilment — customer has no real-time visibility between "Processing" and "Delivered"
No structured supplier communication — phone/email sourcing creates no audit trail and can't be tracked in the admin portal
Shipping label generation is done outside the platform (separate website) — tracking numbers entered manually, error-prone
Local delivery scheduling is entirely ad-hoc — no confirmation system, no fallback if manager is unavailable
Personalisation lead time not communicated to customer at checkout — they expect standard delivery, receive 7–12 day timelines
Sales reporting requires manual reconciliation after every order close — not automatic on status change
